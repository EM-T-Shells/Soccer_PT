HOW THE CYCLE WORKS
• Rotate through Workout A → B → C → D, then repeat. Warm-up is the same every day. • Each day pairs different PT + sprint work so you never hammer one quality two sessions in a row — better for recovery and the knee. • Conditioning is easy Zone 2 (20-30 min) on A, B, C, and one harder Threshold session on D. Keep the Zone 2 days genuinely easy so Day D and your sprint work stay sharp. • Discomfort during adaptation = okay. Sharp pain or swelling = stop.
WHAT'S NEW IN V6
